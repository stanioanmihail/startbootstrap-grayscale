About M4Security

The M4Security device is a bluetooth handsfree that encrypts your phone calls conversation, regardless of the communication technology you use. Thus, it works just as fine on any network: GSM, WiFi, Mobile data, 3G, 4G or other, as long as you connect your M4Security. You can both engage a phone call or receive one, but keep in mind that your partner must also use one in order to establish a secure connection.
Worry not about key management or software updates, as this solution is a black box one; the devices are preconfigured and the keys are being refhreshed with every phone call. Not even us can decrypt your call.
